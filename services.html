Common Questions

How long does it take to build a site?

Student portfolios: 2-7 days. Business sites: 1-4 weeks depending on complexity. Rush delivery available for additional fee.

Do I need to provide content?

Yes - you provide text, images, and information about your business/experience. I can help refine and organize it, but core content comes from you.

What if I need changes after launch?

Each package includes specified revision rounds. After launch, updates are billed at $50/hour or included in monthly maintenance plans.

Will my site work on mobile?

Absolutely. Every site is fully responsive and tested on phones, tablets, and desktops.

Do you offer refunds?

50% deposit is non-refundable once work begins. Remaining balance refundable if not satisfied before final delivery.

Can I update the site myself later?

Yes! I can provide basic training and documentation. For easier self-management, I can build on platforms like WordPress (additional cost).
