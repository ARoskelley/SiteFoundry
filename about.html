About SiteFoundry

Built to help real people get quality websites at fair prices.

Why SiteFoundry Exists

I'm Aiden, a web developer and student at BYU-Idaho. I started SiteFoundry because I kept seeing the same problem from different angles:

Quality websites were either absurdly expensive (agencies charging $5k+ for basic sites) or painfully cheap (DIY platforms that look amateur and limit what you can do). There was no middle ground for people who needed something professional but couldn't justify agency prices.

Students graduating without portfolios. Parents changing careers who needed to showcase new skills. Small businesses paying monthly subscriptions for mediocre sites. Freelancers with great work but no way to show it online.

So I built tools to make professional websites accessible. No bloated agencies. No predatory subscriptions. Just clean, fast, professional sites at honest prices that work for real people.
